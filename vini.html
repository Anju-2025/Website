About Vinicius Junior

Vinicius Junior is a professional footballer from Brazil, playing as a forward for **Real Madrid** and the **Brazil national team**. Known for his speed, dribbling, and finishing skills, he is regarded as one of the best young players in the world.

Career

  • 2014-2018: Flamengo (Brazil)
  • 2018-Present: Real Madrid (Spain)
  • 2019-Present: Brazil National Team
